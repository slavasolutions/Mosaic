A short month. Two pours, one repair, one new pattern.

## Pours

- **April 30** — Six escutcheons in C932 (the "Hello, world" post run). Five clean, one cold shut. Re-melted.
- **May 2** — Four door pulls in C955. All four clean. Slight grain on the underside of pull #3; filed.

## Repair

A pelmet finial came back with a hairline crack near the boss. Brazed it with a low-temperature silver alloy, cleaned the seam, re-polished. Out the door the same week.

## Pattern

A new template for a small **escapement plate** — wax cut, photographed, archived. Not poured yet; the client has not signed off on the drawing. We will not flask until they do.

## What did not happen

We did not pour anything bigger than 200 g this month. The crucible is due for relining and we have been pushing small work through the smaller pot. Relining goes on the schedule for the second week of May.
