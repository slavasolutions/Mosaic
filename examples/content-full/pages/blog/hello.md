Every shop has a notebook somewhere, half full of grease. Ours is on a shelf above the burnout kiln and has been there since 2017. Half of what is in it is wrong and the other half is illegible. So we are doing it again, in public, with dates.

## Three categories

- **Process notes** — small fixes that took us a week to find.
- **Casting logs** — what we poured, what it weighed, what failed.
- **Sourcing** — where the wax, the sand, and the bronze are coming from this season.

We will not write a post unless we have something to put in it. Expect roughly one a fortnight.

## A worked example

Last Thursday we poured a run of six escutcheons in C932. Five clean, one cold shut on the lip. Re-melted #3. The gate was at the top of the lip instead of the side — the metal hit the wall before it filled the detail. That fix went in the shop notebook, on the page after a smudged sketch of an unrelated finial. This time it goes here.
