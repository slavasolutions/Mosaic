A piece of original bronze comes in — chipped, broken, or missing entirely from a set. We replace it.

## How the job runs

1. **Reference.** A rubbing, a photograph, a surviving twin, or all three.
2. **Pattern.** We cut a wax to match. If the original is gone we draw one; the customer signs the drawing.
3. **Pour.** Lost-wax in C932 or C955 depending on the period and the finish wanted.
4. **Finish.** Files, then a chosen patina — natural, statuary, fumed, or matched against a sample.
5. **Inspection.** You see it in person before it leaves the shed.

## What we charge by

Per piece, with a minimum-batch of four. Pattern work is separate and one-time per design. A sample finish on a sacrificial off-cut is included for any new patina.

## What we will not do

- Cast iron. Wrong shop, wrong furnace.
- Plated steel reproductions. We can recommend a finisher who does.
- Volume runs over twenty. Past that, you want a production foundry.
