Sometimes you do not need a new piece — you need the old one fixed.

## What we can do

- **Brazing.** Hairline cracks, broken bosses, separated joints. Low-temperature silver alloy for fine work; bronze brazing rod for heavier joints.
- **Polishing.** Files, scrapers, the slow lathe, and the polishing wheel.
- **Patina matching.** We keep a sample shelf going back a decade; if your patina is a known recipe we probably already have it.

## What we cannot do

- Lacquer work — we send that next door.
- Plating — different shop, different smell.
- Anything involving lead solder. We do not work with lead.

## Pricing

Hourly, with an estimate in writing before we start. Most jobs that come through this door are under three hours.
