A client has a need. There is no off-the-shelf part. We make it.

Typical commissions:

- Door pulls and escutcheons matched to an interior.
- Drawer pulls and knobs for a built-in cabinetry job.
- Curtain pelmet fittings, picture rails, and the harder-to-find decorative fixings.

## How we scope a commission

A short call. A sketch from us, a sign-off from you. Then a small-batch first pour (usually one or two pieces) so you can hold the finish in your hand before we commit the full run.

We do not work on retainer. We bill per project and we are happy to give a fixed quote once the drawing is signed.
