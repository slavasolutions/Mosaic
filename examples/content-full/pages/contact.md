A few notes before you write.

- **Email is best for first contact.** It gives us time to scope the job properly and write you back with a real answer.
- **Pour days are Friday afternoons.** Please do not knock on the green door between noon and four on a Friday.
- **For commissions**, a rough sketch or a reference photograph dramatically speeds up the first response.
- **For repairs**, a photograph of the damage and a second photograph of the whole piece next to a ruler is enough to start the conversation.

If you have a deadline, mention it in the first email. We turn down at least a third of the work we are asked to do because we cannot meet the date — better that we say so on day one than three weeks in.
