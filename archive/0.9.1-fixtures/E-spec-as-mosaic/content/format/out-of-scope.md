# §10. Out of Scope

Explicitly **not** defined by §1–§9 (left to companion documents, profiles,
or consumers):

- Content references between records — see `ref:references`.
- Cascade / inheritance — see `ref:cascade`.
- Routing / URLs, design tokens, redirects — left to a web-oriented
  companion spec to be published separately.
- Validation schemas beyond manifest structure — a schema layer concern.
- Bundling / transport — a consumer concern.

A consumer MAY implement any of these; doing so MUST NOT require deviating
from §5–§9.
